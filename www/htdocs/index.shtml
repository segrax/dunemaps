DuneMaps

DuneMaps is a Dune II Scenario Editor, supporting both Amiga and PC .INI Files aswell as SCENARIOS.PAK.

Based on my previous OD2 Project, and LibEastwood.

 

Certain files from the original Dune2 are required, from either Battle of Arrakis or Building of a Dynasty v1.07 (US & Hitsquad verions). The files are outlined in the Readme.
